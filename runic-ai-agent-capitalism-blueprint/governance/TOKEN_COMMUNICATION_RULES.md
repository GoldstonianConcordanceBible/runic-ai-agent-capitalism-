# Token Communication Rules (Compliance-aware)

Allowed framing:
- community participation
- culture / membership
- contribution signaling
- governance coordination (documentation)

Disallowed framing:
- "investment"
- "guaranteed returns"
- "profit"
- "we will pump"
- price predictions or ROI claims

Required:
- risk disclosure language when discussing high-risk assets
- clarity that nothing is financial advice
- avoid implying profit from efforts of a core team
