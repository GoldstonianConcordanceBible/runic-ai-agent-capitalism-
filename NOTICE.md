# NOTICE – Regulatory & Policy Positioning

This project aligns with:

• SEC Staff statements on digital assets and meme coins
• The Howey "investment contract" framework
• White House digital asset market structure guidance

Key Principles:

1. No expectation-of-profit language
2. No ROI guarantees
3. No investment framing
4. Contribution-first governance
5. Clear risk disclosures
6. Transparent provenance

Tokens discussed in this repository are treated as:

• Community participation artifacts
• Governance signaling tools
• Cultural or membership identifiers

They are NOT represented as:
• Investment vehicles
• Securities
• Yield-bearing instruments

Facts and circumstances determine regulatory status.
Consult counsel.
